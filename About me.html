About Me

Hello! I'm Narinder Pal Singh, a Mechanical Engineer by profession and a passionate book reader and writer. My creation is "Shakti," which is written about the heroes of WW2 and their unspoken story. I love immersing myself in different literary worlds and creating my own stories. Reading has always been my escape, and writing allows me to share my imagination with the world.
